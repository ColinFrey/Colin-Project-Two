Lou Tonkin

Materials: Hand-pulled Linocut on Paper Created: 2018

A detailed blackbird sits in the foreground within a garden. the piece is defined by rythmic, hand-carved textures that create a sense of movement in the grass and sky.

Materials: Reduction Linocut on Japanese Paper Created: 2017
